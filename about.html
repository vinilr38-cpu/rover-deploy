Abstract & Thesis

AgroVision addresses the critical inefficiency of broad-spectrum chemical application in industrial farming. By leveraging custom-trained ResNet-50 models on low-power ARM architectures, we've developed a system capable of real-time pest identification with >99% confidence. Our project proves that precision agriculture doesn't require cloud connectivity, operating entirely within local wireless mesh networks.
