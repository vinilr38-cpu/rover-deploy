Getting Started Guide

1

Setup Raspberry Pi

Flash the AgroVision OS image onto your SD card and insert it into the Raspberry Pi. Power on the device and wait for the status LED to turn green.

[Diagram: Pi Power Connection]
2

Connect Camera

Attach the Pi Camera module to the CSI port or connect a USB camera. Ensure the lens is clear and pointed toward the crop area.

[Diagram: Camera CSI Slot]
3

Login to Portal

Open the AgroVision web app on your mobile or PC. Use your registered farmer credentials to access the main dashboard.

[Interface: Login Screen]
4

Monitor Farm

Navigate to the 'Live Camera' section. You will see real-time video with AI-powered pest detection overlays.

[Interface: Camera Stream]
5

Control Spray

Use the 'Spray Control' panel to manually activate pesticide application or enable 'AI Auto Mode' for autonomous operation.

[Interface: Control Buttons]
6

Download Reports

Go to the 'History' page to review system logs and export findings as CSV or PDF for compliance records.

[Interface: Export Buttons]
