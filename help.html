Frequently Asked Questions

What is the latency of the edge camera stream?

Our system uses h.264 hardware encoding on the Raspberry Pi combined with a WebRTC signaling server to achieve sub-200ms glass-to-glass latency, even in low-bandwidth environments.

Can the system identify pests at night?

Yes, with the optional IR-cut camera module and external infrared illumination, our ResNet-based models are trained to detect silhouettes and heat signatures of common nocturnal pests.

How do I update the AI model on the edge node?

Model updates are delivered as OTA (Over-The-Air) binary patches. Navigate to System Logs > Hardware and click "Sync Weights" when a new version is detected.
